Future Roadmap

Beyond Basic Scoring:
The Next Frontier

NCSS-IQ is designed not just for today's needs but as an evolving platform. Our roadmap extends beyond consumer credit reporting to become a comprehensive financial intelligence hub that powers the future of Iraq's digital economy.

By integrating advanced analytics and broader data networks, we will unlock new value streams for banks and deeper financial inclusion for SMEs and individuals.

Growth Trajectory

SME Credit Intelligence

Specialized scoring models for small businesses utilizing cash-flow data, supply chain payments, and invoice history to bridge the SME financing gap.

Open Banking Infrastructure

Secure API frameworks enabling customers to share financial data with third-party fintechs, fostering a competitive ecosystem of innovative financial products.

AI Lending Recommendations

Machine learning algorithms that provide banks with predictive insights on borrower behavior and personalized product recommendations to maximize lifetime value.

Regional Credit Data Networks

Cross-border credit data exchange protocols with GCC and neighboring countries to facilitate trade finance and support Iraqi businesses expanding abroad.
